`r?say` **Say something trough the bot!** __Usage__: *r?say <message>*
`r?weather` **Shows the weather in a place** __Usage__: *r?weather <place> [country]*
`r?clap` **Makes a text with the :clap:** __Usage__: *r?calp <text>*
`r?mock` **Mocks some text** __Usage__: *r?mock <text>*
`r?urban` **Get the defintion of something** __Usage__: *r?urban <word>*
`r?translate` **Translate somthing in other languange** __Usage__: *r?translate <language> <word>*
`r?rate` **Rate someone** __Usage__: *r?rate <@user>*
